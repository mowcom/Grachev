About

The Chess Institute of California employs leading chess instructors and education professionals to provide and engaging and fun experience for students of all ages.

Our Mission

The Chess Institute of California’s mission is to integrate chess into child learning. Building off the success of programs around North America the CIC is committed to bringing the game of chess to children throughout Southern California.

Founded by Nicholas Grachev, an experienced chess instructor and curriculum developer, and Jeanne Huybrechts, former President of Harvard Westlake whose commitment to education spans more than three decades, the CIC is committed to strengthening academic skills as well as life skills.

Why Chess

Focus

Children are taught the benefits of observing carefully and concentrating.

Analyze

Children learn to evaluate the results of specific actions and sequences.

Plan

Children are taught to develop longer range goals and take steps toward bringing them about.
